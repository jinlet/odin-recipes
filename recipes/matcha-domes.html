Matcha Domes

This amazing Japanese inspired dessert is sure to impress your pallette. The earthly flavors of the matcha combined with the chocolate from the mousse is a great textural experience.

Ingredients:

  • Dark Chocolate
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Gelatin Sheets
  • Heavy Cream
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sugar
  • Matcha Powder

Steps:

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter over a bain marie. When melted, set aside.
  2. Whip the heavy cream into stiff peaks, then slowly fold in the chocolate.
  3. Melt the gelatin sheets and add in the mix with vanilla.
  4. Put the mousse into round molds and freeze for at least 10 hours.
  5. Make the matcha glaze by adding gelatin sheets, matcha powder, heavy cream and sugar in a pot and stir until the matcha powder has dissolved.
  6. When the matcha glaze mixture has reached 37 degrees celcius, take the domes out of the freezer and glaze them.
