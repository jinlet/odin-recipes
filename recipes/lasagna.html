Lasagna

This dish was made by my great great great Italian grandmother. It was a stormy day, the electrons were electrifying. Everything was serene.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Ground beef
  • Water
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Cellery
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Love
  • Tomato Puree
  • Pasta

Steps:

  1. Add garlic, carrots, onions and cellery in a pan and fry until soft
  2. Fry the ground beef until brown
  3. Add tomatoes and water and cook for 4 hours
  4. Season with salt and pepper
